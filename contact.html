Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report a safety concern?
If you have a safety concern, please contact our safety team immediately through the "Safety Concern" option in the contact form above. We take all safety reports seriously and will respond within 24 hours.
How long does it take to get a response?
We typically respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours. For urgent matters, we recommend using our live chat feature or calling our support line.
How can I verify my account?
To verify your account, go to your profile settings and click on "Verify Account". You'll need to provide a valid government ID and a recent photo of yourself.
What are your support hours?

Email Support: 24/7

Live Chat: 24/7

Phone Support: Monday-Friday, 9am-6pm EST

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the exchange work?

Travelers provide help to hosts in exchange for accommodation and sometimes meals. The type of help varies depending on the host's needs and the traveler's skills.

How long can I stay with a host?

Stays typically range from 1 week to 3 months, depending on the host's requirements and the agreement between both parties.

What kind of help can I provide?

Common types of help include farming, teaching, childcare, hostel work, language practice, and various creative or technical skills.

Is there a membership fee?

Yes, we charge a small annual membership fee to maintain the platform and ensure the quality of our community.

Safety Guidelines

Your safety is our top priority. Learn how to stay safe while traveling and hosting.

Emergency Contact

If you're in immediate danger or need urgent assistance, contact our 24/7 safety team:

+1 (234) 567-890

Before You Travel

Verify Your Host

Always check host profiles, reviews, and verification status before accepting an opportunity.

Research Your Destination

Learn about local customs, laws, and safety concerns in your destination country.

Share Your Plans

Inform friends and family about your travel plans and keep them updated.

Get Travel Insurance

Purchase comprehensive travel insurance that covers medical emergencies and trip cancellation.

During Your Stay

Stay Connected

Keep your phone charged and maintain regular contact with friends and family.

Trust Your Instincts

If something feels wrong, trust your gut and remove yourself from the situation.

Document Everything

Keep records of your work hours, agreements, and any issues that arise.

Red Flags to Watch For
  • Hosts asking for money
  • Unsafe living conditions
  • Excessive work hours
  • Lack of clear communication

Host Safety Guidelines

Screen Travelers

Review profiles, references, and communicate thoroughly before accepting travelers.

Set Clear Expectations

Clearly communicate work hours, responsibilities, and house rules in advance.

Provide Safe Accommodation

Ensure living spaces are clean, safe, and meet basic standards.

Respect Boundaries

Maintain professional relationships and respect personal space.

Reporting Safety Issues

Contact Safety Team

Report any safety concerns immediately to our 24/7 safety team.

Document Incidents

Keep records, photos, and messages related to any safety issues.

Local Authorities

Contact local police or emergency services if you're in immediate danger.

Leave Reviews

Share your experience (good or bad) to help other community members.
